WHERE  IT  ALL  BEGAN

Born in Tamil Nadu with a simple idea — make great kulfi and create real opportunities. OH my KULFI was established in 2025 with a vision to redefine indulgence while empowering local entrepreneurs.

Over the years, we have grown to become more than just a dessert stop. We’ve seen the joy our kulfi brings to families and the success it creates for our partners. We are not just a brand; we are a community of dessert lovers and dreamers.
